Best Hawker Foods in Sinagore

Top 3 must try hawker food recommended by Joanna

Chicken Rice

The dish comes with a mountain of fragrant ricecooked with pandan leaves and ginger and spicy chilli,and ginger paste dipping sauces.

Satay

Choice of chicken,pork,beef, or mutton thats seasoned and skewered before getting grilled over a charcoalflame. Its complement with a classic peauntdipping sauce for an expolsion of flavor!

Fried Carrot Cake

Its a mixture made with radish and rice flour, steamed, cut into cubes, and fried with garlic, preserved radish, and eggs.
